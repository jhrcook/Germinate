PagingViewController

class PagingViewController : PageboyViewController

A paging view of general information and notes.

This is the parent view controller for the detail information for a single plant. It has two children view controllers, one for managing the general infomation and another for the notes. This view controller handles most of the navigation, though some is still left to the children when the edits are narrow enough and relevant to the view controller.
